# Day 9: MariaDB Troubleshooting

## Scenario
The Nautilus database team uses **MariaDB** on Stratos DC servers.  
They reported that MariaDB service is not working as expected.  

Your task as a DevOps engineer is to troubleshoot the MariaDB service and make it run correctly.  

Requirements:
1. Identify why MariaDB is not starting.  
2. Fix the configuration or service issue.  
3. Ensure MariaDB runs properly and is enabled on boot.  
4. Verify by connecting with the MariaDB client.
